Cookie Policy.

This policy explains how REN Studio and its service providers use cookies, local storage and similar technologies.

Last updated: July 19, 2026

What these technologies do

Cookies and local storage allow websites and service providers to remember information in a browser. They may be used to operate and secure a service, remember choices, measure performance, prevent abuse, and deliver or measure advertising.

Essential services

REN Studio is hosted by Netlify. Netlify may process technical information and use essential technologies needed to deliver, secure and protect the website. The private Decap CMS administration area may also use authentication storage for authorized editors. These functions are separate from advertising personalization.

Newsletter

When you submit the newsletter form, REN Studio sends the information you provide to its newsletter service, Brevo, through a server-side Netlify function. Brevo processes subscriber information so REN Studio can maintain its mailing list, record consent and provide unsubscribe controls. The form does not require advertising consent.

Google AdSense

REN Studio uses Google AdSense code to verify and, after approval, monetize the website. Google and approved advertising partners may use cookies, local storage, IP addresses or similar technologies to serve and measure ads, limit repetition, detect fraud and abuse, and personalize advertising when permitted by a visitor's choices.

Depending on consent and regional requirements, visitors may receive personalized or non-personalized ads. Learn more through How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and Google's cookie information.

Consent choices

REN Studio uses Google's certified consent management platform for visitors in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland. Where applicable, the message allows visitors to consent, refuse consent or manage individual options. Advertising technologies use the consent signals supplied through that platform.

Managing or withdrawing consent

Where Google's consent message applies, use the Privacy choices link in the website footer to review or withdraw a previous decision. The link is displayed when Google's consent API confirms that European regulations apply. You may also clear cookies through your browser and manage advertising preferences through Google Ads Settings. Blocking storage may affect some website or advertising features.
