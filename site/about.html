The studio

Ideas should become things.

REN Studio is a technology media and maker brand presented by Xiaoyu. We explore the tools changing digital life—and then build with them.

What we cover

Technology, hands-on projects, AI, creator tools, tutorials and honest reviews. REN Studio is intentionally broader than a single trend: the common thread is curiosity put into practice.

How we work

We aim to make unfamiliar technology understandable without pretending it is effortless. Projects should explain the tradeoffs, reviews should identify limitations, and tutorials should help readers produce a real result.

Xiaoyu

Xiaoyu is the presenter and visual identity of REN Studio, connecting the website’s articles and projects with future videos and creator content.

Editorial principles

  • Create original, useful material.
  • Correct meaningful mistakes.
  • Disclose sponsorships and affiliate relationships.
  • Separate opinion from verified fact.
  • Respect safety, privacy and intellectual property.
