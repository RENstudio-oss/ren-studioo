Privacy Policy.

This policy explains what REN Studio may collect, why it is used, and the choices available to visitors.

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Current services

REN Studio uses Netlify for hosting, forms and server-side functions; Brevo for newsletter subscriptions and email delivery; and Google AdSense for site verification and, after approval, advertising. REN Studio does not currently provide public user accounts.

Information we may collect

When you use the contact form, REN Studio receives information you intentionally submit, such as your name, email address, selected subject, consent choice and message. When you join the REN Studio newsletter, we receive your email address, optional first name and marketing-consent choice. Netlify may also process technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, requested pages, form metadata and security logs to host and protect the service.

Newsletter and email delivery

Newsletter subscription information is sent through a Netlify server-side function to Brevo. Brevo processes subscriber details so REN Studio can maintain its mailing list, send requested updates, record marketing consent and provide unsubscribe controls. Subscribers may unsubscribe through the link included in each marketing email.

Cookies and local storage

REN Studio and its providers may use cookies, local storage and similar technologies for essential operation, security, consent management and advertising. See the Cookie Policy for details and available controls.

Advertising and Google AdSense

REN Studio uses Google AdSense code for site verification and, after approval, advertising. Third-party vendors including Google may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses or similar identifiers to serve, personalize and measure advertising. Google and its partners may serve ads based on visits to this and other websites when allowed by the visitor's consent and applicable law.

Visitors can manage personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings. Learn how Google processes data on partner sites at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

For visitors in the EEA, UK and Switzerland, REN Studio uses Google's certified consent management platform. Where it applies, visitors can consent, refuse consent, manage options and later revisit their decision through the Privacy choices link in the footer.

How information may be used

  • To operate, secure and improve the website.
  • To respond to messages that visitors send.
  • To send REN Studio projects, stories and creator updates to people who explicitly subscribe.
  • To display and measure advertising after approval and in accordance with applicable consent choices.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse.

Sharing and retention

Information may be processed by providers needed to host, secure, communicate through or monetize the website. REN Studio does not sell personal information. Data should be kept only as long as needed for the stated purpose, security, recordkeeping or legal obligations.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability or objection. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe from future marketing messages at any time using the unsubscribe link included in each campaign. Requests can also be submitted through the Contact page. Where available, use the Privacy choices footer link to revisit Google consent settings.

Children

REN Studio is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children below the age required by applicable law.

Changes

This policy may be updated when services, laws or business practices change. The date above identifies the latest version.
