Privacy Policy.

This policy explains what REN Studio may collect, why it is used, and the choices available to visitors.

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Current service status

REN Studio currently uses Netlify for hosting and contact-form processing. It does not currently run Google AdSense, audience analytics, or public user accounts. Advertising-related sections below apply only after those services are enabled.

Information we may collect

When you use the contact form, REN Studio receives information you intentionally submit, such as your name, email address, selected subject, consent choice and message. Netlify may also process technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, requested pages, form metadata and security logs to host and protect the service.

Cookies and local storage

This preview stores only your cookie-banner choice in your browser. See the Cookie Policy. If analytics or advertising is enabled later, this policy and the consent controls must be updated before those technologies load where consent is required.

Advertising and Google AdSense

If REN Studio activates Google AdSense, third-party vendors including Google may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses or similar identifiers to serve, personalize and measure advertising. Google and its partners may serve ads based on visits to this and other websites.

Visitors can manage personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings. Learn how Google processes data on partner sites at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Where required, advertising technologies will be blocked until the appropriate consent choice is obtained through a Google-certified consent-management platform.

How information may be used

  • To operate, secure and improve the website.
  • To respond to messages that visitors send.
  • To understand site performance after appropriate analytics settings and consent are configured.
  • To display and measure advertising after AdSense and required consent controls are enabled.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse.

Sharing and retention

Information may be processed by providers needed to host, secure or operate the published website. REN Studio does not sell personal information. Data should be kept only as long as needed for the stated purpose, security, recordkeeping or legal obligations.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability or objection. Once a real contact address is configured, requests can be submitted through the Contact page. You may also change advertising preferences through Google Ads Settings and adjust browser cookie controls.

Children

REN Studio is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children below the age required by applicable law.

Changes

This policy may be updated when services, laws or business practices change. The date above will identify the latest version.
