# REN Studio v2 Preview

A simplified responsive homepage for REN Studio with a black-and-red identity, animated Xiaoyu hero, one featured project, three latest stories, and a compact topic strip.

## Files

- `index.html` — page structure and text
- `style.css` — design and responsive layout
- `script.js` — mobile menu, Xiaoyu pointer animation, and current year
- `images/` — clearly labelled placeholder artwork
- `privacy.html`, `terms.html`, `cookies.html`, `disclaimer.html` — policy pages
- `about.html`, `contact.html` — trust and contact pages
- `legal.css` — shared policy-page and cookie-notice styling

## Preview it on your computer

1. Extract the ZIP.
2. Open the extracted `REN-Studio-v1` folder.
3. Double-click `index.html`.
4. It opens in your normal web browser.

If fonts do not appear, make sure you are connected to the internet. The page otherwise works offline.

## Replace the images

The easiest method is to keep the filenames:

1. Prepare your replacement image.
2. Give it the same filename as the placeholder you want to replace.
3. Put it inside the `images` folder and approve replacing the old file.

You can also use JPG, PNG, or WebP. If the extension changes, update the matching `src="images/..."` path in `index.html`.

Recommended image shapes:

- `xiaoyu-hero-v3.png`: corrected portrait with unified face, hair, clothing, and background lighting
- `xiaoyu-workshop-placeholder.svg`: landscape, about 4:3
- project images: landscape, about 16:10

## Publish free with Netlify Drop

1. Extract `REN-Studio-v1.zip`. Do not upload the unopened ZIP.
2. Go to `https://app.netlify.com/drop`.
3. Sign in or create a free Netlify account.
4. Drag the entire extracted `REN-Studio-v1` folder onto the upload area.
5. Wait for Netlify to finish. It gives you a public `netlify.app` address.
6. Open that address to confirm the site is live.

## Choose a nicer free address

1. Open your site in the Netlify dashboard.
2. Open **Site configuration**.
3. Find **Change site name**.
4. Try a name such as `ren-studio-media` or `renstudio-builds`.
5. If available, your address becomes `https://your-name.netlify.app`.

## Publish future changes

1. Edit the files in your extracted folder.
2. Preview `index.html` again.
3. In Netlify, open the site's **Deploys** page.
4. Drag the updated folder into the manual deploy area.

## Important preview behavior

- Navigation and mobile menu work.
- Xiaoyu gently floats and reacts to pointer movement. Reduced-motion settings are respected.
- Article and project links marked `#` are placeholders for future pages.
- The contact form is a preview and does not transmit messages. Connect it to a monitored inbox before launch.
- The cookie notice remembers a local choice but is not a certified advertising CMP.

## Before applying for Google AdSense

1. Replace all placeholder article/project links with original, useful published content.
2. Connect the Contact form to a real monitored inbox and add accurate owner/business details where required.
3. Update the policies to match the exact hosting, analytics, advertising, newsletter and form providers actually used.
4. Add AdSense only after approval and configuration.
5. For personalized ads served in the EEA, UK or Switzerland, configure a Google-certified consent-management platform through AdSense **Privacy & messaging** or another certified provider. The included preview banner is not a substitute.
6. Review the final policies with a qualified professional for the countries and audience you serve. These templates are a starting point, not legal advice.
